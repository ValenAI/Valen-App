**Valen** by GrowthFlux allows you to permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the app.

## Steps

1. Open the Valen app
2. Go to **Profile** (bottom navigation)
3. Tap **Security**
4. Tap **Delete Account**
5. Type **DELETE** to confirm

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is **permanently removed**:

- Your account and authentication records
- Profile information (name, email, avatar, date of birth)
- All goals, tasks, and milestones
- All chat messages and conversation threads
- All reflections and outcomes
- All achievements and progress
- Your profile photo
- All session and refresh tokens

This action is **irreversible**. Once confirmed, your data cannot be recovered.

## Deleting Data Without Deleting Your Account

If you want to keep your account but clear your content:

1. Go to **Profile → Security**
2. Tap **Clear My Data**

This removes all your goals, tasks, chats, and achievements while keeping your account active.
